Our Story

Nintendo arose from a simple dream: to bring joy to every home through play. Founded in Kyoto in 1889, the company began with handcrafted playing cards modest symbols of imagination that would one day evolve into entire worlds of adventure. Over decades, that small spark of creativity grew into a vision that transcended generations, inspiring millions to laugh, compete, and explore together.

From the early consoles that defined living room entertainment to the handheld devices that shaped childhoods around the world, Nintendo has never stopped reinventing the meaning of fun. Through every challenge, every innovation, and every leap into the unknown, we remained guided by one belief: that play connects people beyond language, age, and borders. It was this spirit that led to the birth of characters and stories beloved across time from Mario’s boundless optimism to Zelda’s timeless courage. These creations were not mere games, but portals into imagination, born from craftsmanship and passion.

We learned that joy, like adventure, is infinite when shared. And so, our journey continues. In a world that moves faster each day, we still hold onto what truly matters creativity, curiosity, and connection. For every new console, every world yet to be discovered, and every smile sparked by play, we remember where it all began: a simple card, a small idea, and a boundless belief in fun without limits. This is Nintendo our story, ever unfolding.
